About Rujex Pro CV

Rujex Pro CV is a lightweight web tool built to help job seekers quickly create professional resumes. It was created to be fast, privacy-friendly, and optimized for the US job market.

How it works

  1. Fill your details in the Resume Builder.
  2. Generate a preview and make edits as needed.
  3. Download the PDF — ready to send to employers or upload to job portals.

Free and privacy-first

All resume data stays in the browser. Downloads are generated locally — no server storage.

Privacy Policy

This tool does not collect or store personal data on servers. Data you enter is saved only in your browser (localStorage) to allow editing and download. If you clear your browser cache or storage, the data will be removed.

Ads shown on this page are served by third-party networks (Adsterra). We do not control ad content. For privacy questions about ads, contact the ad provider.
